How to make Lumpia

Panlasang Pinoy style

Ingredients

  • 50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 3 cups cooking oil (for frying)

Filling Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 2 pieces onion minced
  • 2 pieces carrots minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup parsley chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 eggs

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, combine ground pork, onion, carrots, parsley.
  2. Add eggs, soy sauce, garlic powder, and black pepper. Mix well
  3. Lay a lumpia wrapper on a flat surface.
  4. Scoop about 1-2 tablespoons of the mixture and place it on the wrapper.
  5. Roll the wrapper tightly and seal the edge with water.
  6. Repeat until all mixture is used.
  7. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  8. Fry the lumpia until it golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove from pan and drain excess oil.
  10. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
